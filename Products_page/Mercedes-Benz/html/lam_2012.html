Mercedes-Benz

Looking for an affordable entry point to the world of luxury cars? The A-Class is the smallest and least expensive Mercedes-Benz, positioned below the CLS-, C-, E-, and S-Class four-doors. Although Mercedes-Benz has been building A-Classes since 1997, this is the first generation available in North America after being introduced for the 2019 model year. The A-Class competes with other entry-level subcompact luxury sedans including the Audi A3, BMW 2 Series Gran Coupe, and Cadillac CT4.
